Space Dream Demo

基于UE4 和Oculus SDK开发的一个Sapce Walk 虚拟现实项目

This Space Dream Demo is free to download for PC & VR at https://www.SpaceDreamVR.com

Space Dream is a Virtual Reality Music Visualizer, designed as an interactive art-piece that lets you fly through space to your favorite songs!
The demo is compatible with the Oculus Rift, HTC Vive, and also works on a standard Desktop Monitor without a VR Headset.

It was created by one individual (Justin Davis), and a full experience with multiple worlds is currently in development.

To follow updates on the game, check out my facebook at https://www.facebook.com/spacedreamvr/

You can also visit my portfolio website at www.Davis3D.com
Feel free to contact me privately if you wish to know more about the game!
